Welcome to tellmonifa

Emotions are like the tides, they ebb and flow. When a tide is high— It’s utter bliss, every moment is filled with smiles, laughter and tenderness. If a tide starts to flow back out— It's misery, in those moments you could probably use some much-needed inspiration and pearls of wisdom that will re-light the embers from some of the smartest people on the planet. That’s why we’ve created tellmonifa, a community of experts who have some nuggets to share with you on how to curb your emotional trauma.
